About US

People who are not able to get credit cards are still able to use financial services through mobile e-wallets.

Financial services offered by e-wallet companies is growing rapidly, particularly, in places where banking services can not reached, in the same way, services based on transportation payment methods are also being developed into such e-wallets.

For the last 4 years, GLN has been working on the development of a network that connects e-wallets from around world

The world became one after the internet was introduced. GLN’s goal is to create a payment network using smart phones wherever the internet is available

It is not required to install a new application to use the service. GLN uses API or SDKs to connect to its partners’ e-wallet.

GLN launched its mobile offline payment services in Taiwan and Thailand and it will expand its presence in the Asia continent to create a Asia Payment Hub by 2020. Ultimately, GLN’s vision is to become a Global Payment Hub by 2025.
