What is GLN?

GLN is a Global Payment Hub platform
that links digital platforms of major
financial institutions, retailers and loyalty point
operators around the world to one
global network, allowing them to freely
transfer/pay with/withdraw2 digital assets.1

  • 1Digital assets are defined as reward points, miles or coupons from airlines or retailers, as well as digital currencies backed by financial institutions.
  • 2Offline Payment Service for Thailand and Taiwan was launched in may 2019. Overseas remittance brokerage service and ATM withdrawal service is under development to open in the first half of 2020.
